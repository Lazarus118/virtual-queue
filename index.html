How it works

    Enter number

  • The user enters their phone number on a tablet (or computer) at the respective location, they are then placed in a queue, where we keep track of their position and average expected wait times.

    • Notify

  • Upon successfully entering the queue, they are notified of how many people are ahead and how many minutes/hours they can expect to wait before it is their turn.

    • Checkoff

  • On the administrative side, which basically will be similar to a task list, the administrator or representative will “check­off” the customers whose turns have already passed.
